
Circle example
--------------

Here is the simple markup which shows circle::

  .. openstreetmap:: Example OpenStreetMap
     :id: circle
     :location: 38.898014,-77.036501
     :zoom: 14

     "The White House", location: 38.898014,-77.036501
     "Near The White House", circle: 38.898014,-77.036501
